# ISO/IEC 27001:2022 Annex A — Selected Controls (Regulatory Vault Excerpt)

## A.5 Organizational controls

- A.5.15 Access control — logical separation of tenant and role boundaries.
- A.5.36 Compliance with policies — documented attestation against security policies.

## A.8 Technological controls

- A.8.12 Data masking — protection of sensitive data in non-production and ingestion paths.
- A.8.16 Monitoring activities — continuous monitoring, anomaly detection, and incident logging.
- A.8.24 Use of cryptography — integrity and confidentiality for regulated data at rest and in transit.
