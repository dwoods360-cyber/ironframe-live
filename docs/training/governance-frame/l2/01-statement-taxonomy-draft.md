# L2 Ch.1 — Statement taxonomy in drafts

> **Track:** GF_L2 · **Lab:** GF-W-001  
> **Manual:** [statement-taxonomy.md](../manuals/statement-taxonomy.md)

## Lab

Write a **400–600 word** classroom draft (topic from trainer: evidence failure, dollar risk communication, or regulatory change).

1. Every paragraph tagged in margin: fact / interpretation / example / recommendation / product relevance / open question.  
2. Zero blended “this law requires…” without a cite.  
3. No product CTA.

Save as `gf-writer-draft-v1.md` in your portfolio folder (not public publish).
