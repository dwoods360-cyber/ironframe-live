## GRC knowledge (apply from manuals)

Board notes should sound like exception / risk language: control · issue · impact · owner · next step — not a sales pitch.

## Lab — Five-sentence board note (ANALYST-004)

Write in your journal (or a shared doc your trainer names):

1. **Context:** tenant + date.
2. **Hazard:** one sentence from Chapter 1 (include threat or vulnerability word).
3. **Evidence:** one sentence from Chapter 2 (design vs operating if you can tell).
4. **Export:** ready / blocked + why (Chapter 3).
5. **Next step:** one concrete ask + **owner role** (control owner / risk owner / trainer).
6. **Residual risk:** one honest line (still open / reduced / unknown).
