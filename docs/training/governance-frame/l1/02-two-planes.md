# L1 Ch.2 — Two planes: research vs product docs

> **Track:** GF_L1 · **Lab:** GF-R-002  
> **Routes:** `/gf-research` (or research host) **and** `/docs`

## Why this matters

Mixing planes creates false evidence and salesy “research.” Readers must keep them apart.

| Plane | Job | Example |
|-------|-----|---------|
| **Governance Frame** | Industry governance research | Briefing on evidence failure patterns |
| **Ironframe `/docs`** | How to operate the product | Student Core / Analyst manuals |
