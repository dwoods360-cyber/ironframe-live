# Chapter 3 — Money integrity (cents)

> **Track:** PRACTITIONER_CORE · **Reading level:** 11th–12th grade · **Release:** `v0.1.0-ga-epic17`  
> **Primary route:** `/integrity` · **Lab IDs:** PCORE-003

## Why this chapter matters

Ironframe stores money as **whole cents** (BigInt). Practitioners read **formatted dollars** in the UI and never invent floats for ALE.

## Learning objectives

- Open `/integrity` and find ALE / exposure dollars.
- Explain “whole cents in storage, dollars on screen” in one sentence.
- Spot a seed baseline vs a workspace ALE your trainer describes.
