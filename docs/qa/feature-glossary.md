# 📖 Sovereign Platform Feature Glossary & Screen Guide
## Interactive Reference Manual for Students and Analysts

Use this guide to understand exactly what each piece of the dashboard does, where to find it on your monitor, and how to operate it during your lab work.

---

## 🗂️ 1. Command Center Dropdown (Tenant Switcher)

<a id="command-center"></a>

* **Screen Location:** Top-left corner of the primary dashboard header panel.
* **What It Is Used For:** Safely swapping the entire software view between three distinct mock corporate environments (**Medshield**, **Vaultbank**, and **Gridcore**) without leaking private data across company walls.
* **How to Use It:**
  1. Click the dropdown box displaying the current company name.
  2. Select a different company from the list.
  3. Watch the center grid panels flash a gray skeleton box and update with new statistics.

---

## 🔋 2. Sustainability Telemetry (Sustainability Pulse)

<a id="sustainability-telemetry"></a>

* **Screen Location:** Top-right quadrant of the dashboard workspace layout.
* **What It Is Used For:** Monitoring the real-time energy efficiency, grid strain, and carbon footprint intensity of the active company's cloud hardware.
* **How to Use It:**
  1. View the live chart inside the panel block labeled **`SUSTAINABILITY PULSE`**.
  2. Check the top right of that box to verify if the orange **`FALLBACK ACTIVE`** badge is glowing.
  3. Change companies using the top-left menu to see how the environmental data shifts automatically.

---

## 🕵️ 3. 19-Agent Workforce Monitor

<a id="agent-workforce-monitor"></a>

* **Screen Location:** Dedicated tracking table/list layout within the lower control deck space.
* **What It Is Used For:** Tracking your automated team of specialized AI micro-workers (like `Ironlock` or `Ironguard`) as they police the network for cybersecurity threats.
* **How to Use It:**
  1. Scan the agent roster list to check their operational statuses (e.g., Green "ACTIVE" or Yellow "WARNING").
  2. Click directly on any individual agent row.
  3. Watch the **GRC Meta Drawer** slide out smoothly from the right side of the screen to read their specific background directives.

---

## 📊 4. Analyst Portal (Ironquery CSV Export)

<a id="analyst-portal-export"></a>

* **Screen Location:** Located inside the administrative management tabs panel labeled **`ANALYST PORTAL`** or **`INTEGRITY HUB`**.
* **What It Is Used For:** Compiling and exporting flawless financial and technical ledger sheets for compliance auditors.
* **How to Use It:**
  1. Navigate to the portal view and find the action panel button labeled **`Export Tabular Ledger Data (CSV)`**.
  2. Click the button to start an instant download stream.
  3. Open the file on your machine and confirm that all financial balances display as exact whole cents (no decimals).
