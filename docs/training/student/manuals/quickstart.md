# Quick-start — Ironframe Core (classroom)

**Track:** Student · **Reading level:** 11th grade  
**Audience:** Classroom students with trainer-issued credentials

This guide gets you into Ironframe Core and oriented on the Command Deck. It is not the design-partner sales path.

---

## 1. Sign in

1. Open the classroom host your trainer gave you (often `http://127.0.0.1:3000/login`).
2. Sign in with your **student credentials**.
3. Confirm you land in a workspace (Integrity Hub or dashboard).

If login fails, stop and ask your trainer. Do not invent admin passwords.

---

## 2. Find the Core nav

After sign-in, look for these areas (names may appear in the top bar or side nav):

| Area | Route | What you do |
|------|-------|-------------|
| Integrity Hub | `/integrity` | Read ALE / protection posture |
| Dashboard home | `/` | Overview of the Command Deck |
| Evidence Vault | `/evidence` | Open sealed evidence views |
| Cockpit | `/cockpit` | Watch agent / workforce activity |
| Board report | `/board-report` | Board-ready risk narrative |
| Trust Center | `/trust` | Trust / procurement materials |
| Exports | `/exports` | Download tenant CSV/PDF when unlocked |
| Docs | `/docs` | Open this student track |

---

## 3. First five-minute scan

1. Open `/integrity`. Name the three panels (left, center, right).
2. Open `/evidence`. Confirm the page loads without error.
3. Open `/docs/training/LEVEL1-STUDENT-INDEX`. Bookmark it for labs.
4. Open the [glossary](./glossary.md) and rewrite ALE and WORM in your own words.

---

## 4. Rules for class

- Keep a lab journal: **route · what you saw · time**.
- PILOT pages are demo data — see [pilot vs preview](./pilot-vs-preview.md).
- Seed money baselines are training fixtures, not real customer dollars.
- Do not open Ops Hub or sales Approvals for Core labs.
