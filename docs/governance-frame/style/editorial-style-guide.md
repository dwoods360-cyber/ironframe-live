# Governance Frame Editorial Style Guide

Authoritative tone and voice rules: [`../charter/editorial-standards.md`](../charter/editorial-standards.md) §10.

Voice: calm, analytical, practical, institutional, evidence-first, restrained.

Avoid hype, fear-based marketing, product evangelism, and SEO “Top 10” formats without genuine research purpose.

Commentary metaphors (e.g. “heatmap theater,” “checklist industrial complex”) must be moderated or labeled as commentary, not fact.
