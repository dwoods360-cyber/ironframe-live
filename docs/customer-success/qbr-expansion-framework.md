# QBR & Expansion Framework

**Classification:** `customer-success` · Quarterly business reviews and land-and-expand

## QBR agenda (60 minutes)

1. **Outcomes recap** (10 min) — controls covered, evidence exports, ALE trend in cents.
2. **Health & adoption** (10 min) — health score band, pilot gates, analyst active days.
3. **Commercial insight** (15 min) — Challenger-style reframe: cost of status quo vs command post.
4. **Roadmap alignment** (10 min) — Shipped / Pilot / Roadmap honesty from docs hub.
5. **Mutual success plan** (15 min) — OKRs for next quarter with single owner per metric.

## Expansion signals (expansionFinder)

| Signal | Expansion play |
|--------|----------------|
| Health ≥ 80 + MSSP profile | Additional enclave tenant or seat pack |
| HEALTH_HIPAA + high evidence | Irontally framework pack add-on |
| UTILITY_NERC + board exports | Utility / NERC sustainability module (sector play — not a demo tenant name) |
| REGIONAL_BHC + CLOSED_WON &gt; 12 mo | Enterprise Ironguard RLS audit expansion |

## Land-and-expand sequence

1. Land — single beachhead use case with measurable first action.
2. Adopt — 90-day onboarding success milestones (see onboarding-success-playbook.md).
3. Expand — QBR-proposed module only after health ≥ 80 for two consecutive poll cycles.

## Anti-patterns

- Quoting expansion ARR without CFO-approved packaging doc.
- QBR without pre-read sent 48 hours ahead.
- Teaching generic GRC theory instead of tenant-specific metrics.
