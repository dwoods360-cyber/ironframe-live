## Prerequisites

- Sales-assisted invite with workspace slug (public self-registration is off)
- Browser: Chrome, Edge, or Firefox (latest)
- Access to `https://{your-slug}.ironframegrc.com`

## Signing in

1. Open `/register/{token}` from your invitation email and set your password.
2. Sign in at `/login` on your tenant host.
3. Accept MSA/DPA, complete Path B billing if PENDING, then finish `/get-started` gates.

## Tenant scope

Stay on your assigned design-partner slug. Seed tenants (`medshield`, `vaultbank`, `gridcore`) are engineering fixtures only.
