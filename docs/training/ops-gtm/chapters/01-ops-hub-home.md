# Chapter 1 — Ops Hub home

> **Track:** OPS_GTM · **Reading level:** 11th–12th grade · **Release:** `v0.1.0-ga-epic17`  
> **Primary route:** `/dashboard/operations` · **Lab IDs:** OPSGTM-001

## Why this chapter matters

Ops Hub is the internal home for workforce, CRM, calendar, briefings, and GTM tools. It is **not** the partner tenant cockpit.

## Learning objectives

- Open Ops Hub without a 403 (or record deny).
- Name three tabs or quick links you can see.
- Find **Operator library** and **workflow-review** links if present.

## How to get there

1. Sign in as the operator role your trainer assigns.
2. Open `/dashboard/operations`.
3. Scan overview tiles (approvals counts, workforce, calendar).
