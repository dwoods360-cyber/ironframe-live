# L2 Ch.3 — Regulatory precision

> **Track:** GF_L2 · **Lab:** GF-W-003  
> **Authority:** GF-STANDARDS-001 §6

## Rules to apply

- Law ≠ guidance ≠ proposal ≠ commentary  
- Final ≠ in force (check dates)  
- Jurisdiction and sector limits  
- Roles (controller/processor/covered entity) not interchangeable  
- No “the world must…” when only one regime applies  

## Lab

1. Find three regulatory mentions in your draft (or add them carefully).  
2. For each: instrument type · jurisdiction · in-force status · role named.  
3. Rewrite any imprecise sentence.
