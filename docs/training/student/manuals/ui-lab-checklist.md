# Ironframe Core — UI lab checklist (student)

**Track:** Student · **Reading level:** 11th grade  
**Purpose:** Click-through checks for Core SaaS screens. No Ops Hub. No sales DISPATCH.

Keep a journal: route · what you saw · pass/fail · time.

---

## Lab A — Layout & panels

1. Sign in and open `/integrity`.
2. Name left (~22%), center (~48%), and right (~30%) panels.
3. Resize the browser. Confirm panels stay usable and scroll independently.

**Pass:** Three panels visible; no blank white crash.

---

## Lab B — Tenant context (classroom)

1. Find the tenant / company control near the top of the workspace.
2. Switch between two seed tenants your trainer allows (for example Medshield ↔ Vaultbank).
3. Confirm numbers or labels change with the tenant.

**Pass:** Data changes with tenant; UI does not freeze.

---

## Lab C — Sustainability pulse (if shown)

1. Find **SUSTAINABILITY PULSE** (or Ironbloom) on the right panel.
2. Note one intensity or status value.
3. Switch tenant once and note whether the value updates.

**Pass:** Panel loads; you can describe one reading in your journal.

---

## Lab D — Evidence & exports

1. Open `/evidence` — page loads.
2. Open `/exports` — page loads (or shows a clear setup banner).
3. Write which gate appeared, if any.

**Pass:** Both routes respond; no uncaught error screen.

---

## Lab E — Docs hub

1. Open `/docs/training/student`.
2. Open [glossary](./glossary.md) and [LEVEL1-STUDENT-INDEX](../../LEVEL1-STUDENT-INDEX.md).
3. Confirm headings and links work.

**Pass:** Student section manuals open in `/docs`.

---

## Out of scope

Skip cookie DevTools drills, Ops Hub, Approvals, and Ironleads unless your trainer assigns a separate ops elective. Those are not Ironframe Core student labs.
