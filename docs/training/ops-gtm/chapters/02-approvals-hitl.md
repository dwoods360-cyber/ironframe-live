## Why this chapter matters

Nothing outbound sends without a human. Approvals is where you edit, DISPATCH, or PURGE drafts.

## Learning objectives

- Open Approvals with the SALES filter.
- Tell PENDING draft from already-sent history (as shown).
- Define DISPATCH vs PURGE in plain words.
- Check message locks: $4,999 · workflow review CTA · no free PoC.

## Lab — Read before send (OPSGTM-002)

1. Open the newest PENDING SALES draft (trainer lab draft only).
2. Checklist: $4,999 present? workflow-review CTA? no free pilot? no seed tenants as customers?
3. Write DISPATCH vs PURGE.
4. **Do not** DISPATCH to a real cold prospect unless the trainer runs a dry-run to your inbox.
