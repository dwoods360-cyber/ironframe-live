## Why this chapter matters

Governance Frame holds published briefings. `/docs` holds Ironframe Core manuals. They are separate planes. Do not mix them.

## Learning objectives

When you finish, you can:

- Open `/governance-frame` while signed in.
- Open one published briefing slug.
- State the difference between Governance Frame and `/docs`.

## How to get there

1. Open `/governance-frame` (login required).
2. Open the published briefing index.
3. Select one briefing under `/governance-frame/[slug]`.
4. Open `/docs` in another tab and compare purpose.

## Lab — Two planes, two jobs (BRIEF-001)

1. Open one briefing on `/governance-frame`.
2. Open one Level 1 manual on `/docs`.
3. Write two columns: Briefing plane vs Docs plane.
4. Add one allowed use and one forbidden mix-up for each.
5. Confirm you did not try to edit either corpus from the UI.

## Check your understanding

- [ ] I can open a briefing.
- [ ] I know `/docs` is the operator handbook plane.
- [ ] I know briefings are not where Day-1 cockpit work lives.

## Common mistakes

- Never assume a briefing rewrite updates `/docs` manuals.
- Classroom students read. They do not author published briefings.
