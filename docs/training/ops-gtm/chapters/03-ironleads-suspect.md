## Why this chapter matters

Ironleads harvests early signals into **SUSPECT** rows. Operators read blockers before SalesTeam drafts.

## Learning objectives

- Open the Ironleads portal from Ops Hub.
- Open one SUSPECT report (or list row detail).
- Name one blocker or enrichment field (website, phone, buying committee) if shown.
