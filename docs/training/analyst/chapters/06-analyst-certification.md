## Why this chapter matters

Certification proves you can run a junior Core workflow under supervision — not that you are a full GRC manager.

When you finish, you can:

- Show **practice exam ≥ 80%** (Knowledge gate K5).
- Show Pass marks for Chapters 1–5.
- Restate ALE, WORM, tenant isolation, CIA, four risk treatments, and `/exports` purpose.
- Submit a packet your trainer signs.
