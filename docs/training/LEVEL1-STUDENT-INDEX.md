# Level 1 Training Index — Student Track (11th grade)

**Audience:** Classroom students and internal trainers. **Reading level:** 11th grade. **Release:** `v0.1.0-ga-epic17`.

Design partners should use the curated [LEVEL1-PARTNER-INDEX](./LEVEL1-PARTNER-INDEX.md) instead. Chapters 09, 11, and 12 include classroom seed labs. Those steps are not partner Day-0 handoff.

Work the chapters in order. Each chapter is a short teach-and-do lab. Keep a simple journal. Write the route. Write what you saw. Write the time.

---

## Before you start

1. Get student credentials from your trainer.
2. Use the classroom host. It is often `http://127.0.0.1:3000` unless your trainer gives another URL.
3. Skim [pilot vs preview](/docs/user-manuals/pilot-vs-preview). Do not treat PILOT pages as live records.
