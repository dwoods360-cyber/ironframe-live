# Quick-Start Activation & Onboarding Guide (Level 1)

**Reading level:** 11th grade · **Milestone:** v0.1.0-ga-epic17 · **Mode:** Sales-assisted invite only

This guide walks you through activating your workspace and learning the main screen layout — without technical jargon.

---

## 1. Receiving and processing your invitation

Ironframe blocks public self-registration to protect infrastructure boundaries. Onboarding is **invite-only** through your sales or delivery team.

1. **Locate your welcome message** — Check your corporate inbox for email from **delivery@ironframegrc.com**.
2. **Open the secure workspace link** — Click the button labeled **Initialize Workspace** (or equivalent in your invite). This establishes a temporary security handshake through the authorization gate.
3. **Configure your credentials** — Choose a strong password. This binds your identity to your organization's compliance profile.

**Common workspaces:** `medshield`, `vaultbank`, `gridcore`, or design partner `pilot-corp`.

If email delivery is delayed, provisioning may show `invitePending: true` while your tenant row is created — contact delivery to resend.

---

## 2. Completing the legal agreement sign-off

Before you view sensitive business metrics, you must accept liability boundaries.

1. On first login, you land on the **Legal Terms Portal** (Master Services Agreement and Data Processing Addendum).
2. Read how your data is isolated from other companies (multi-tenant privacy boundaries).
3. Type your **full legal name** and click **Accept Agreements**. The system records an immutable audit entry.

---

## 3. Basic interface navigation

After authorization, you enter the **Command Post Dashboard**. The layout supports **keyboard navigation** — press **Tab** to move between controls. Charts include text summaries for screen readers.

```text
====================================================================================
 [Top Bar Navigation]  Dashboard Cockpit  |  Integrity Hub  |  Evidence Locker  | Docs
====================================================================================
 [Active Tenant Window]  Currently Displaying: (your assigned tenant)
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  FINANCIAL TARGETS (Safe Baselines)          HAZARD PIPELINE (Real-Time Risks)
  - Medshield Status:  $11,100,000.00         - Active Threats Locked: 0
  - Vaultbank Status:  $5,900,000.00          - Automatically Cleaned: 14
  - Gridcore Status:   $4,700,000.00          - Structural State: Operational
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 [Accessibility: Tab to navigate. All charts have text summaries.]
====================================================================================
```

**Alt-text (wireframe):** Top navigation bar with three functional areas below — financial protection baselines on the left, live hazard pipeline on the right, current tenant name centered.

---

## Primary control areas

| Area | What it does |
|------|----------------|
| **Integrity Hub** | Financial risk scores and protection status |
| **Workforce Cockpit** | Automated safety sweeps and agent activity |
| **Evidence Locker** | WORM-locked compliance documents (cannot be deleted after seal) |
| **Documentation** | In-app reader for this manual set |
| **Settings** | Tripartite keys, contacts, tenant configuration |

---

## Billing hold

If subscription payment fails, a **Billing Gate** blocks the Command Center. Use **Update Payment Method** on the billing hold screen, or contact delivery@ironframegrc.com.
