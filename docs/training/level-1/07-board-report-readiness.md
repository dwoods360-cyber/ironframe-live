## Why this chapter matters

Leaders need a short readiness picture. Board Report gathers posture for executive review.

## Learning objectives

When you finish, you can:

- Open `/board-report` and scan readiness sections.
- Compare board signals with Integrity Hub.
- Capture a classroom snapshot or notes for submission.

## How to get there

1. Open `/board-report`.
2. Read the posture or readiness sections shown.
3. Compare any DORA or framework percentage with `/integrity`.
4. Save notes or a screenshot for your trainer.

## Lab — Executive readiness notes (REPORT-001)

1. Open `/board-report` and `/integrity` in two tabs.
2. Write three bullet findings for a fictional board sponsor.
3. Include at least one dollar or readiness number from Integrity Hub.
4. Include one risk or agent observation from earlier labs if you have it.
5. Keep the write-up under 120 words.

## Check your understanding

- [ ] I can open `/board-report`.
- [ ] I can explain why executives want this view.
- [ ] I can keep findings short and concrete.

## Common mistakes

- Long paste dumps are not board-ready. Prefer three clear bullets.
- Do not invent DORA numbers that are not on screen.
