# Governance Frame Conflicts of Interest

Authoritative detail: [`editorial-standards.md`](./editorial-standards.md) §§1, 16, 19.

Commercial relationships, product references, and potential conflicts must be disclosed. Financial, professional, and product conflicts of outside contributors and reviewers should be disclosed where appropriate. Editorial decisions remain separate from Ironframe sales and product-marketing objectives.
