# Chapter 2 — Evidence chain for one risk

> **Track:** ANALYST · **Reading level:** 11th grade · **Release:** `v0.1.0-ga-epic17`  
> **Primary route:** `/evidence` · **Lab IDs:** ANALYST-002

## Why this chapter matters

Auditors ask “show me proof.” The Evidence Vault holds sealed records. **WORM** means sealed items are not quietly deleted.

## Learning objectives

When you finish, you can:

- Open `/evidence` for the active tenant.
- Link one evidence item (or vault view) to the hazard you chose in Chapter 1.
- Explain WORM in one plain sentence.

## How to get there

1. Keep your Chapter 1 hazard note open.
2. Open `/evidence`.
3. Find a sealed or sealable record your trainer allows you to inspect.
4. Note any WORM / seal language on screen.

## Lab — Evidence link (ANALYST-002)

1. Journal the evidence route and record label (or “empty vault — trainer noted”).
2. Write how this evidence would support (or not yet support) your Chapter 1 hazard.
3. Write a one-sentence WORM definition in your own words.
4. Note period vs point-in-time: do you have dated proof across time, or only one view today?
5. Do not delete sealed records.
