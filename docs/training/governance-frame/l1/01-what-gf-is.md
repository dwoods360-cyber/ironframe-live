# L1 Ch.1 — What Governance Frame is (and is not)

> **Track:** GF_L1 · **Reading level:** 11th grade · **Lab:** GF-R-001  
> **Primary route:** `/gf-research` or `https://research.ironframegrc.com`  
> **Authority:** `docs/governance-frame/charter/what-governance-frame-is.md`

## Why this matters

Employers want people who can tell **research** from **product marketing**. GF is research / executive education — vendor-neutral.

## Learning objectives

- State what GF is in one sentence.
- List three things GF is **not**.
- Explain the GF → industry → Ironframe relationship (Ironframe almost never the article subject).

## How to get there

1. Open `/gf-research` (or the public research host).
2. Open **What Governance Frame Is** / About.
3. Skim positioning and “what it is not.”
