# Retention Playbook — Ironframe Customer Success

**Classification:** `customer-success` · Retention & save motions

## When to intervene

| Signal | Threshold | Play |
|--------|-----------|------|
| Stale engagement | No CRM interaction in 30+ days | Re-engagement advisory |
| Critical silence | No interaction in 60+ days | Executive save call draft |
| Low evidence completeness | Pilot metadata &lt; 70% | Guided evidence workshop |
| Gate B miss | Consecutive pilot gate failures | Operator escalation + remediation plan |
| Config churn spike | LP-10 notifications elevated | Integrity walkthrough offer |

## Save play structure (SB7-adapted for existing customers)

1. **Empathy** — acknowledge their board pressure and audit calendar.
2. **Guide authority** — cite tenant-scoped TAS integrity and Irontally control coverage.
3. **Plan** — three concrete steps with dates (not feature dumps).
4. **Call to action** — single meeting or evidence upload target.
5. **Stakes** — dollar-denominated exposure from Irontrust (whole cents only).

## Anti-patterns

- Promising certification outcomes Ironframe has not shipped.
- Blaming the customer for low adoption without operator review of onboarding gaps.
- Expansion pitch while health score is below 60.
- Auto-sending save emails without operator co-sign.
